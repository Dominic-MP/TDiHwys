Innovation Hub —
Recently Scanned Documents

This tool is used to display the newest images that were scanned in the Innovation Hub and are now in the National Archives catalog.

It is configured to use the National Archives catalog API to display the first page's thumbnail from each of the most recent 192 descriptions with digital images that have been tagged as scanned by a "citizen contributor".
